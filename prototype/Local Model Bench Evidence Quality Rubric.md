## Purpose
Use this after the next real trial to grade local-model benchmark evidence before promoting claims.

## Evidence quality checklist
- [ ] Model, quantization, hardware, prompt set, and runtime flags are recorded.
- [ ] Results include pass/fail outputs and latency/cost notes.
- [ ] Baseline comparison is captured against at least one non-local model or previous run.
- [ ] Decision separates “usable for toy tasks” from “safe for real repo work”.
